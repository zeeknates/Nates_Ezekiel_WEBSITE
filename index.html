SEASONAL ITEM:BIBINGKA

Bibingka, with its dense yet moist texture and subtle coconut sweetness, becomes a culinary centerpiece during Christmas celebrations. This iconic treat symbolizes more than just a culinary delight; it encapsulates the spirit of togetherness, sharing, and the warmth of Christmas in the hearts and homes of Filipinos nationwide.
